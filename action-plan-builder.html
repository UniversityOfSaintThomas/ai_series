AI Ethics Action Plan Builder

Create a comprehensive action plan for implementing ethical AI practices at your institution. This tool will help you assess your current state, identify priorities, and develop specific action items with a focus on both ethical considerations and environmental sustainability.

How to Use This Tool

  • Complete the self-assessment to evaluate your current AI ethics practices
  • Identify priority areas based on your assessment results
  • Develop specific action items for each priority area
  • Set timelines and assign responsibilities for implementation
  • Generate a complete action plan that you can download and share

AI Ethics Self-Assessment

Governance & Oversight

Assess your institution's current AI governance and oversight practices:

1. Does your institution have a formal AI ethics policy or guidelines?

2. Is there a designated person or committee responsible for AI ethics oversight?

3. How frequently are AI ethics policies reviewed and updated?

Education & Training

Assess your institution's AI ethics education and training efforts:

1. Do faculty and staff receive training on ethical AI use?

2. Are students educated about AI ethics and responsible use?

3. Are resources available to help with ethical AI implementation?

Environmental Sustainability

Assess your institution's consideration of AI's environmental impacts:

1. Does your institution consider the environmental impact of AI systems?

2. Are there guidelines for energy-efficient AI use?

3. Is AI's environmental impact included in procurement decisions?

Implementation & Monitoring

Assess your institution's AI ethics implementation and monitoring practices:

1. Are AI systems assessed for ethical risks before implementation?

2. Is there ongoing monitoring of AI systems for ethical issues?

3. Is there a process for addressing ethical concerns when identified?
