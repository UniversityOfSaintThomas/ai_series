AI Ethics Syllabus Builder

Create a customized AI ethics policy for your course syllabus by selecting the components that best fit your needs. This tool will help you develop clear guidelines for AI use in your courses while addressing key ethical considerations.

How to Use This Tool

  • Expand each section to view available policy components
  • Check the boxes for components you want to include
  • Customize the text in the provided fields as needed
  • Preview your complete AI ethics policy at the bottom
  • Copy the final text to include in your syllabus
