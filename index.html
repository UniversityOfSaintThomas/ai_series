Welcome to the AI Ethics Course

This 75-minute micro-credential course is designed to provide faculty and staff at the University of St. Thomas with a comprehensive understanding of AI ethics principles, challenges, and best practices for implementation in higher education.

Course Overview

This course consists of five 15-minute modules covering essential aspects of AI ethics in higher education, with a special focus on sustainability and environmental impacts. Each module includes interactive elements, multimedia content, and knowledge assessments to enhance your learning experience.

What You'll Learn:

  • Fundamental AI ethics principles and frameworks
  • AI risks, biases, and privacy considerations
  • Environmental and sustainability impacts of AI
  • Responsible AI integration in teaching and research
  • Creating effective AI ethics action plans

Course Features:

  • Interactive case studies with ethical dilemmas
  • Environmental impact dashboard visualizations
  • Knowledge checks with immediate feedback
  • Syllabus builder for creating AI policies
  • Action plan builder for ethical AI implementation
